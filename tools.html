Tools

The various tools described in this document help estimate fuel consumption, emissions and Noise to better analyse environmental impacts, address interdependencies and facilitate constructive exchanges with aircraft operators and local communities. The tools have been designed to objectify pollution (not nuisance) and the emission of gaseous pollutants, and to create a solid basis for further steps. None of the software packages are meant to “judge”, they just provide factual outputs based on the inputs made by the users. Some of the tools focus on the analysis of completed flights, while others are designed for the calculation of planned procedures. Some applications offer both functionalities, while others include the capability for Noise impact assessments.

Note: the information presented in this section has been collected via presentations made to the sub-group by experts from the organisations providing the tools or using them, and/or from information publicly made available on the websites of those organisations. Since the document targets experts, and to avoid making it unreadable, it does not provide the explanation of the acronyms in the description of the tools or dashboards itself. The acronyms can be found in Section 7.

EUROCONTROL Tools: Fuel Burn Scope and User Mapping
Tool Primary users Scope Aggregation Phase Context
AEM (Advanced Emissions Model) Analysts, researchers, ANSPs, policy support Physics-based estimation of fuel burn and emissions Flight / trajectory level, scalable to aggregates Supports gate-to-gate Methodological analysis, emissions modelling, research and policy support
SET (Small Emitters Tool) Aircraft operators (small emitters); verifiers/administering States (EU/UK/Swiss ETS context) Statistical estimation of fuel burn and associated CO₂ emissions for flights within ETS reporting scope (statistical model) Flight list / operator fleet level (supports totals over lists of flights) Entire flight as represented by the tool (based on statistical models and flight characteristics) ETS monitoring and reporting support; data-gap reporting; simplified monitoring for eligible operators
IMPACT Network Manager, analysts Consistent network-wide fuel burn and emissions estimates Pan-European network level Typically airborne Network performance monitoring, reporting, trend analysis
FlyingGreen ANSPs, States, airports, Policy makers (module-dependent) Fuel burn and emissions indicators derived from multiple data sources (using AEM) Configurable: ANSP, airport, State, or network views Phase-dependent, module-specific Transparency, stakeholder dialogue, awareness, decision support

Note: Fuel burn values from different tools should not be directly compared without understanding scope and assumptions. No single tool provides a complete representation of ANSP environmental performance. Interpretation must always reflect the intended user and decision context.

Strategic / Management

Advanced Emission Model (AEM)

Suite for calculating aircraft engine emissions across 4D trajectories (LTO + CCD), supporting ~24 pollutants (CO₂, NOx, SOx, PM, HC, …). BADA and CAEP methodologies, CLI/GUI/API variants.

Details
Tactical / Planning

Open‑ALAQS

Airport Local Air Quality Studies: QGIS plugin for inventory & dispersion (AUSTAL), covering aircraft and non‑aircraft sources (roads, GSE). Public code, flexible source selection.

Details
Tactical / Planning

IMPACT & INTACT

Web platform for Noise, gaseous and particulate assessments (BADA4 TEM). ICAO/ECAC‑aligned. INTACT enables automated, near‑real‑time local impact maps and graphics.

Details
Operational / Expert

NEST

Network Strategic Tool for scenario‑based planning and airspace design via DDR datasets. Simulates trajectories, CAPA/ENV/Cost‑EFF impacts; AIRAC‑aligned updates.

Details
Operational / Expert

R‑NEST

Research‑grade network simulation tool (CASA delay model, 3D HMI) for concept validation, scenario comparison and SESAR projects; very high performance.

Details
Strategic / Management

ACROPOLE

AI‑based fuel flow & burn from radar/ADS‑B (QAR‑trained proxies; ICAO EDB/BFFM2 for emissions). Supports indicators (CDO/CCO) and ops inefficiency analysis.

Details
Operational / Expert

AEDT

FAA’s 4D aviation environmental design tool for fuel, emissions, Noise & dispersion; scalable (airport→global). Uses ANP, BADA3/4, ICAO EDB; Windows + SQL Server.
