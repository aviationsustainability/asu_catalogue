AVENIR Pillar 2 Catalogue

An open-source resource for Air Navigation Service Providers (ANSPs). It helps teams across all organisational levels — from operational experts to senior management — to understand, select, and apply tools and methodologies that improve environmental performance in ATM. The catalogue is non‑prescriptive, practical, and easy to navigate, avoiding any perception of assessment, ranking, or compliance pressure.

The scope

The AVENIR Working Group (WG) explores how Air Navigation Service Providers (ANSPs) can enhance environmental performance through the adoption of new technologies and operational procedures. A central objective of the group is the exchange and structuring of best practices, particularly regarding how the environmental impact of operational changes is measured, analysed, and communicated.

Within this framework, AVENIR WG developed a shared knowledge repository, a catalogue of tools and methodologies used to evaluate fuel consumption, emissions, noise, and their interdependencies.

The catalogue serves as a practical reference for ANSPs by:

  • Providing a structured overview of available analytical and modelling solutions
  • Supporting transparent and data-driven assessment of environmental impacts
  • Promoting knowledge sharing and methodological consistency across organisations
  • Reducing duplication of effort
  • Assisting in the selection of tools suited to specific operational environments
