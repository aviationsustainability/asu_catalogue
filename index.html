AVENIR Pillar 2 Catalogue

An open-source resource for Air Navigation Service Providers (ANSPs). It helps teams across all organisational levels — from operational experts to senior management — to understand, select, and apply tools and methodologies that improve environmental performance in ATM. The catalogue is non‑prescriptive, practical, and easy to navigate, avoiding any perception of assessment, ranking, or compliance pressure.

The scope

One of the core goals of the AVENIR Working Group (WG) was to identify methods and provide insight into how ANSPs can improve environmental performance through new technologies and operational procedures. By focusing on the exchange of best practices — including how the impact of operational changes is measured and how performance trade‑offs are addressed — Pillar 2 set out to create a shared, open repository of this knowledge. Based on contributions made between 2023 and 2025, including presentations, workshops, and operational case studies, the catalogue offers ANSPs a comprehensive overview of existing solutions for evaluating fuel consumption, emissions, noise, and their interdependencies. It promotes knowledge sharing, reduces duplication of effort, and helps organisations choose tools that best align with their specific operational environment.
