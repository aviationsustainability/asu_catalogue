Avenir Pillar 2 Catalogue

The AVENIR Working Group was established to explore how Air Navigation Service Providers (ANSPs) can enhance environmental performance through the adoption of new technologies and operational procedures. A central objective of the group was the exchange of best practices, particularly regarding how the environmental impact of operational changes is measured, analysed, and communicated — including the management of associated trade-offs.

Within this framework, Pillar 2 focused on developing a shared knowledge repository. Drawing on contributions between 2023 and 2025 — including expert presentations, workshops, and operational case studies — the group compiled a catalogue of tools and methodologies used to evaluate fuel consumption, emissions, noise, and their interdependencies.

The catalogue serves as a practical reference for ANSPs by:

  • Providing an overview of available analytical and modelling solutions
  • Supporting objective, data-driven assessment of environmental impacts
  • Promoting knowledge sharing and consistency across organisations
  • Reducing duplication of effort
  • Assisting in the selection of tools suited to specific operational environments

Open‑Source Governance: The Avenir Catalogue is developed as an open‑source, collaborative reference. Its evolution is driven by operational experience, user feedback, and contributions from ANSPs, ensuring that methods, tools and practices remain aligned with real-world challenges and emerging needs.
