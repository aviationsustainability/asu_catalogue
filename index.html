AVENIR Environmental Catalogue

Find tools, dashboards and operational use cases supporting environmental performance in ATM.

This catalogue brings together existing tools, dashboards and operational use cases used across Europe to support environmental performance monitoring, analysis and improvement in Air Traffic Management (ATM). It provides a practical entry point to help users navigate available solutions and understand how they can support operational, analytical and reporting activities within ANSPs.

Who is this for?

This catalogue is intended for ANSP teams who want to:

  • Monitor environmental performance
  • Analyse ATM inefficiencies and fuel burn
  • Support environmental reporting
  • Evaluate operational or technical measures
  • Explore monitoring dashboards
  • Learn from practical ANSP use cases and methodologies

Fuel Burn Results

How to Interpret Outputs from Different EUROCONTROL Tools and Dashboards

Several EUROCONTROL aviation environmental tools provide fuel burn and CO₂-related outputs. These values are sometimes interpreted as directly comparable. In practice, differences in results are expected and arise mainly from differences in methodology, scope, system boundaries, level of aggregation and intended use of each tool/dashboard.

About AVENIR

The catalogue was developed under AVENIR Working Group as a shared knowledge repository for ANSPs. It is intended as a practical, non-prescriptive resource supporting knowledge sharing, transparency and easier navigation across available environmental tools and practices.
